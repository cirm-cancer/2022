Registration fees
The workshop itself is FREE. Participation however requires that applicants stay on site at CIRM, mostly in shared twin rooms. These include full boarding (3 meals), and cost 95€ per day.
More information on the accomodation is available on the CIRM website here and there.

Scolarships
According to the number of final participants, we may be able to offer a few scolarships to cover the full board stay in a shared room at CIRM. Some additional bursaries may eventually become available in the last days prior to the meeting.
If you are interested, or depend on such a help to cover your participation, please apply by sending a cover letter to the organizers (pierre DOT martinez AT lyon DOT unicancer DOT fr).
